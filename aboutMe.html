I like to make and play video games! It's what I live for. No exaggeration. I've been playing games since I was... three maybe? I probably didn't know what I was doing but I knew I liked it.

It was mostly retro games, or things that were as old as me. I'm pretty sure the Wii is about the same age as me. I played a lot of GameCube games too. And some older stuff through the Wii virtual console.

As I got older I started tinkering with making my own games. I used a bit of Scratch, and messed around in MCreator too. Both of them use block coding, the second of which is for making Minecraft mods.

Eventually I joined the OCVTS computer science program and learned a lot about making games and web development. I made this website myself!
