The Process

  • It all started when I was in the shower thinking about what I would do for this project. I thought about maybe making a top-down action game like one I had seen another student make, or a platformer similar to a small project I had made earlier on in the year. I abandoned that project after learning that you can't easily do camera stuff in MakeCode Arcade.

  • Then my ADHD decided to start thinking about how I wanted to play Satisfactory again, and then the 2 thoughts collided in my head to form the wonderful idea of making my own factory building game. Little did I know, it would be quite the endeavor.

  • I started with the Codecademy course for all of Phaser. It included a large portion of the learn JavaScript course, which I had already completed.
